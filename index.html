Communication Cables

Deep in the ocean there are "submarine cables" (communication lines) that carry our phone and internet
traffic. The first submarine cables were laid in the 1850s to carry telegraphy.
Using data obtained from TeleGeography combined with the Mapbox JavaScript library.
The map below shows all communication lines laid in the 1850s, not necesarily if they are still in service.
Pan the map around, right click with the mouse to move the map in different directions. Zoom with the mouse's scroll wheel.
Find x,y coordinates by hovering the mouse around the map.
